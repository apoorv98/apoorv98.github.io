You can also transfer to participating frequent travel programs like United MileagePlus, Hyatt Gold Passport, etc. For example, you can transfer the points 1:1 ratio to Hyatt points, which you can use to book Hyatt hotels and the points are values at least 1.5 cents/point. Essentially, the 55,000 points you get from openning the card are valued at least $825 if you consider booking hotel using Hyatt or $687.5 if you booking travel through Chase. $0 Introductory Annual Fee the first year, then $95 after the first year.
